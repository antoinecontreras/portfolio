ANTOINE CONTRERAS (B.1998, france) IS AN INDEPENDENT GRAPHIC DESIGNER, BASED BETWEEN paris & An interaction designer graduate of ECAL, Antoine is passionate about visual creation and digital interaction. His work is characterized by close collaboration with other creatives, integrating diverse disciplines such as web design, motion design in 2D and 3D, as well as 3D modeling. Antoine has recently worked with artists and brands in the fashion and music industries, contributing to notable projects such as the promotional campaign for Mode Suisse and art direction for the magazine Athletica. Additionally, he collaborates with studios in Paris, continuously enriching his creative approach. To learn more about his background and achievements, please visit his website. Antoine is available for any questions or potential collaborations.
